What We Do

Here is our specific process that ensures every project is a success.


  • Research
  • Prototyping
  • Build
  • Launch


Research

Before we start to build your website, we firstly try and understand your business and its target market. Once we know how the business operates and who its customers are, we can then start setting some goals on what we actually want the website to do.



Prototyping

Once our goals are set, we can then start planning the web development and design process. We do this by setting up a prototype using one of various wireframing programs so that you can see the basic page structure, color schemes and graphics.



Build

If you are happy with our prototype mockup, we then begin building the site. Throughtout development we check if adjustments are needed ensuring that the best user experience is achieved on all devices and web browser platforms.



Launch

Upon website completion, we begin testing making sure that all UI elements are functioning as they should be and that the site works on all devices and browsers. Once testing is complete, we will then send the site to the client to review before going live.
